F.A.Q's

HOW DO I KNOW WHICH RESUME SERVICE PACKAGE IS RIGHT FOR ME?

If you're focused on optimizing your profile and gaining interview support, the Career Success and Career Launch packages provide excellent value. The Career Starter is ideal for those who need a foundational, ATS-optimized resume and minimal extras. We offer guidance to help you decide the right fit for your situation.

WHAT DOES THE “TAILORED FOR SPECIFIC ROLES” FEATURE MEAN IN MY PACKAGE?

It means your resume will be customized to reflect the key skills, experiences, and keywords relevant to the specific role you’re targeting. We focus on crafting resumes that highlight your experience in alignment with the expectations of the job market, ensuring you meet employer priorities for the positions you're aiming for.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO RECEIVE MY FINISHED RESUME?

The standard turnaround time is 5-7 business days. For faster delivery, we offer an expedited service upon request. Our team ensures quality within this timeframe, focusing on a resume that aligns with your career goals.

CAN I REVISE MY RESUME AFTER RECEIVING IT?

Yes! Our resumes are designed to be easily updated as your career evolves. We provide guidance on how to make changes, and you can always reach out to us for advice on tweaking your document for specific applications.

WHY SHOULD I INVEST IN A PROFESSIONAL RESUME WHEN I CAN DO IT MYSELF?

While you can certainly write your own resume, our professional writers bring industry-specific knowledge, expertise in crafting ATS-friendly resumes, and a clear understanding of employer expectations. This significantly increases your chances of landing interviews, saving you time and effort that would otherwise go into trial-and-error resume writing.

WILL MY RESUME BE REJECTED BY ATS (APPLICANT TRACKING SYSTEMS)?

No, ATS do not automatically reject resumes. They are designed to help hiring teams manage large numbers of applications more efficiently by parsing resumes for relevant keywords, qualifications, and skills. However, ATS do prioritize resumes that are properly formatted and include the right keywords for the job. While ATS may rank resumes based on their relevance to a job description, your resume still needs to be compelling to human reviewers.

At MissFit, we optimize your resume for ATS compatibility, ensuring it’s formatted correctly, includes important keywords, and is structured for maximum impact. This way, your resume is likely to pass through ATS filters and still stand out when it reaches the recruiter or hiring manager.

WHAT’S INCLUDED IN THE LINKEDIN PROFILE OPTIMIZATION AND PERSONAL BRANDING TUTORIALS?

Our LinkedIn Profile Optimization and Personal Branding Tutorial ensures your online presence aligns with your professional goals. We’ll guide you on how to effectively showcase your skills, experience, and accomplishments in a way that increases visibility and appeal to recruiters.

HOW DO I ACCESS THE CAREER COACHING SESSIONS AND MOCK INTERVIEWS?

Once you’ve selected your package, we will schedule your coaching and mock interview sessions directly. These sessions are personalized to your career goals, and you’ll get expert advice on your interview strategy, how to answer common questions, and specific preparation for the roles you’re targeting.

WILL THIS RESUME GUARANTEE ME A JOB?

While we can’t guarantee job offers, we can guarantee that our resumes are crafted to meet employer expectations and increase your chances of standing out in a competitive job market. Our goal is to position you as the top candidate and provide you with the tools, support, and confidence to succeed in your job search.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I DON’T GET THE JOB I WANT AFTER USING YOUR SERVICE?

We provide continuous support to help you improve your application strategy. If you're facing challenges, you can reach out for additional guidance. Our team is committed to helping you succeed, offering career coaching, resume updates, and personalized advice as needed.

WHAT SUPPORT DO I GET AFTER I RECEIVE MY RESUME?

Our service doesn’t end once you receive your resume. Depending on the package you purchase, you’ll have access to email support (12 months for Career Mastery, 6 months for Career Success, etc.), which means you can reach out with questions, adjustments, or new job opportunities. Additionally, all packages include a subscription to Career Insider, with weekly advice and resources to help guide your career.

WHY IS THE PRICE DIFFERENT FOR EACH PACKAGE?

The price varies based on the level of personalization, support, and additional services included in each package. Higher-tier packages offer more resume versions, extended coaching, and longer email support, ensuring you have a comprehensive solution for your job search.
