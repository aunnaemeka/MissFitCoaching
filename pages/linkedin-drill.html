F.A.Q's

WHO IS THIS PROGRAM FOR?

The LinkedIn Drill is designed for professionals who want to take control of their career narrative and unlock new opportunities using LinkedIn. Whether you’re job hunting, building your personal brand, or seeking to expand your professional network, this program is for you.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE PROGRAM?

The program includes:

  • Personalized LinkedIn profile optimization.
  • Strategies for creating impactful content.
  • Tools and scripts for strategic networking.
  • Weekly workshops with expert guidance.
  • Access to templates, analytics tools, and a support community.

HOW LONG IS THE PROGRAM?

The LinkedIn Drill is a 3-month program with weekly workshops and actionable tasks to help you build a powerful LinkedIn presence.

WHAT IS THE TIME COMMITMENT?

Participants should allocate 3-5 hours per week, which includes attending workshops, completing exercises, and applying strategies on LinkedIn.

HOW MANY PARTICIPANTS ARE IN THE WORKSHOP?

To ensure personalized attention, the program is limited to 20 participants per session.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I MISS A WORKSHOP?

All workshops are recorded, and participants will have access to recordings and materials. However, active participation is strongly recommended to maximize the program’s benefits.

CAN I JOIN IF I ALREADY HAVE A GOOD LINKEDIN PROFILE?

Absolutely. Even if your profile is optimized, this program will help you refine your personal brand, enhance your content strategy, and master networking to achieve your career goals.

DO I NEED SALES NAVIGATOR TO BENEFIT FROM THIS PROGRAM?

No, Sales Navigator is not required. While the program includes tips on using Sales Navigator effectively, the strategies are designed to work with or without it.

IS THIS PROGRAM FOCUSED ONLY ON JOB SEEKERS?

Not at all. While job seekers will benefit greatly, the program is also tailored for professionals looking to build a personal brand, attract leadership opportunities, or expand their network strategically.

WHAT SUPPORT IS AVAILABLE DURING THE PROGRAM?

You’ll receive weekly workshops, personalized feedback, access to outreach templates and guides, and ongoing support through our community platform.

ARE PAYMENT PLANS AVAILABLE?

Yes. We offer flexible payment options:

  • $750/month if paid upfront.
  • $850/month for installment payments.
  • $600/month early-bird pricing.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THE PROGRAM ENDS?

After the program, you’ll have access to all templates, guides, and recorded sessions to continue applying the strategies. You’ll also leave with a fully optimized profile, a content strategy, and a strong professional network.

HOW DO I KNOW THIS PROGRAM WILL WORK FOR ME?

The LinkedIn Drill is built on proven frameworks and strategies that have helped countless professionals achieve tangible career growth. If you’re committed to the process, you’ll see results.

HOW DO I SECURE MY SPOT?

Click "Get Started" to enroll. Remember, spots are limited to 20 participants per session, so early registration is recommended.
