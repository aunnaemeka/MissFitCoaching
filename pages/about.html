THE CAREER GYM FOR PROS.

MissFit Coaching provides tailored career coaching for job seekers, employees, and executives. Our approach is built on decades of experience in corporate HR and leadership, combined with a deep understanding of what it takes to thrive in today’s competitive landscape.

THE MISSFIT STORY

A philosophy born from 30 years of experience, reshaping how careers are guided—tailored, personal, and designed for those who dare to be different.

MissFit Coaching was born from a single, striking realization: while organizations are equipped with teams, tools, and strategies to succeed, individuals are often left to navigate their careers alone, armed only with outdated advice and generic resources.

Dee, the founder of MissFit Coaching, spent over 30 years in corporate HR and leadership roles at global organizations like Google, Gallagher, and Analytle Health. During her time in these roles, she saw the immense disparity between the resources available to employers and those available to job seekers, professionals, and executives alike.

This imbalance left a lasting impression. Over the years, Dee began to imagine what career growth could look like if individuals were given the same level of support, insight, and strategy that companies rely on to thrive. That idea grew into what we now call the MissFit philosophy: the belief that each career path is unique and deserves a tailored approach, one that honors the goals, values, and aspirations of the individual.

The name MissFit itself reflects this belief—a nod to the many talented people who don’t fit neatly into traditional molds, but whose potential shines when given the right guidance and tools.

MissFit Coaching isn’t just about careers; it’s about meeting people where they are, understanding their unique challenges and ambitions, and helping them move forward with clarity and confidence.

This story is where it all began—a journey to level the playing field and create a space where every individual, regardless of their starting point, can thrive on their own terms.

Our principles guide everything we do, ensuring a personalized, transparent, and growth-focused approach to help you achieve lasting career success.
