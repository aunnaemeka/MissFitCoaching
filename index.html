F.A.Q's

WHAT CAN I EXPECT FROM A COACHING SESSION?

In a coaching session, you can expect personalized attention from your coach, who will actively listen to your goals and challenges. Sessions include insightful discussions, constructive feedback, and actionable tips tailored to your unique situation. Many clients find that sessions are not only informative but also invigorating, leading to immediate mindset shifts and practical applications.

HOW DOES THE COACHING PROCESS WORK?

Our coaching process starts with an initial consultation to understand your goals and values. You'll complete a questionnaire that helps us discover your strengths and challenges. Sessions are flexible and can be scheduled at your convenience. Throughout the process, you will receive regular check-ins, detailed recaps of each session, and ongoing support to ensure consistent progress.

WHAT ARE THE QUALIFICATIONS OF YOUR COACHES?

With over 30 years of leadership experience, including a decade as a corporate VP and global work across top companies like Verizon and Google, Dacia “Dee” DeRidder brings deep, real-world expertise to your career journey. She combines tailored strategies with actionable insights, offering proven solutions that elevate your career to new heights. This isn’t just guidance—it’s the strategic expertise you need to drive real progress and unlock your full potential.

WILL I RECEIVE PERSONALIZED MATERIALS?

Yes! We offer customized materials, including tailored resumes and cover letters designed to highlight your strengths and fit the specific roles you're interested in. Many clients have reported receiving positive feedback on their revamped job applications, leading to interview invitations and job offers.

HOW QUICKLY CAN I EXPECT RESULTS?

While results vary based on individual circumstances, many clients experience a significant shift in their mindset and career direction after just one session. Our comprehensive coaching programs are designed to help you make steady progress, and you can expect practical results, such as improved job applications and interview preparation, within weeks.

WHAT IF I'M NOT SURE WHAT MY CAREER GOALS ARE?

That's completely normal! Our coaching process includes exercises and discussions designed to help you identify your values, strengths, and the types of roles that align with them. We focus on self-discovery to empower you to set meaningful and achievable career goals.

DO YOU OFFER GROUP COACHING SESSIONS?

Yes, we provide group coaching sessions that create a supportive community for participants. These sessions foster relationship-building and allow you to learn from others' experiences while receiving guidance from our coaches.

HOW DO I SCHEDULE A COACHING SESSION?

You can easily schedule a coaching session after you have been onboarded. Simply choose a time that works for you, and we will accommodate your schedule. If you prefer, you can also reach out to our team via phone or email for assistance.

WHAT IF I NEED TO CHANGE MY APPOINTMENT?

We understand that life can be unpredictable! You can reschedule your appointment with at least 24 hours' notice. Our team is committed to providing flexible options to accommodate your needs.

IS THERE A SATISFACTION GUARANTEE?

We are confident in the quality of our coaching services and strive to provide an outstanding experience for all our clients. If you feel that our coaching isn't meeting your expectations, please reach out to us, and we will work with you to address your concerns.
